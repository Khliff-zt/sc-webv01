Student Leadership

Students have many opportunities to develop leadership skills through various roles and organizations.

  • Student Council: Elected representatives who organize events and advocate for students.
  • Peer Tutoring Program: Students helping fellow students succeed academically.
  • Community Service Club: Organizing volunteer opportunities and service projects.
