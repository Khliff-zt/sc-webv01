Welcome to the Parents Community

At Siha Science & Technology School, we believe that parents are our most important partners in education. We value your involvement and support in your child's academic journey.

Parent-Teacher Association (PTA)

Our PTA is a vibrant community of parents and teachers working together to enhance the educational experience of our students.

PTA Objectives:
  • Support educational programs and activities
  • Facilitate communication between parents and school
  • Organize community-building events
  • Raise funds for school improvement projects
