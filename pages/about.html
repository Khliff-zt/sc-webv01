Our Mission

To provide quality education and empower students to achieve their full potential in the fields of science and technology.

At Siha Science & Technology School, we believe in:

  • Academic excellence through innovative teaching methods
  • Nurturing curiosity and critical thinking skills
  • Developing responsible global citizens
  • Fostering an inclusive and supportive learning environment

Our History

Founded in 2005, Siha Science & Technology School began with a vision to transform science and technology education in our region. What started as a small institution with just 50 students has now grown into a premier educational institution serving over 500 students.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation in teaching methodologies and curriculum development, always staying at the forefront of educational trends and technological advancements.
