Our Academic Programs

At Siha Science & Technology School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to prepare students for success in fields of science and technology.

Elementary Education (Grades 1-5)

Our elementary program focuses on building strong foundations in mathematics, science, and technology while developing essential literacy skills.

  • Hands-on science experiments
  • Advanced mathematics curriculum
  • Introduction to coding concepts
  • Comprehensive literacy program

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Our middle school program builds on elementary foundations with more advanced coursework and specialized STEM subjects.

  • Advanced science courses (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
  • Robotics and engineering projects
  • Computer programming
  • Project-based learning

High School (Grades 9-12)

Our high school program offers college-preparatory coursework with specialized tracks in various STEM disciplines.

  • Advanced Placement (AP) science courses
  • Computer science and AI
  • Engineering and design
  • Research opportunities
