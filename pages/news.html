New Sports Facilities Opened

September 28, 2023 | Campus Improvements

We are excited to announce the opening of our new sports facilities, which include a state-of-the-art gymnasium and outdoor fields. These facilities will enhance our students' physical education programs and provide more opportunities for sports and recreation.

The new gymnasium features a professional-grade basketball court, volleyball court, climbing wall, and fitness center. The outdoor facilities include a soccer field, track, and tennis courts.

Annual Science Fair Announcement

September 15, 2023 | Academic Events

Join us for our Annual Science Fair on March 15th. Students will showcase their innovative projects and experiments. This year's theme is "Sustainable Technologies for the Future."

Parents and community members are encouraged to attend. The event will take place in the main hall from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

Upcoming Parent-Teacher Conferences

September 10, 2023 | Parent Information

Parent-Teacher Conferences will be held on October 10th and 11th. Please sign up for a time slot to discuss your child's progress with their teachers.

Students Win Regional Robotics Competition

August 25, 2023 | Student Achievements

Congratulations to our Robotics Team for winning first place at the Regional Robotics Competition! The team's innovative design and programming skills impressed the judges and secured them a spot in the national competition.

Team members Sophia Chen, Marcus Johnson, and David Kim worked tirelessly for months on their robot design. Their dedication and creativity paid off with this remarkable achievement.
