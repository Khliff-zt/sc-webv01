Admissions Process

We welcome students from all backgrounds who have a passion for science and technology.

Steps to Apply

  1. Submit Application
    Complete our online application form with all required information.
    Step 1
  2. Documents Submission
    Upload academic records, recommendation letters, and other required documents.
    Step 2
  3. Entrance Examination
    Complete our assessment in mathematics and science.
    Step 3
  4. Interview
    Participate in an interview with our admissions committee.
    Step 4
  5. Decision Notification
    Receive admission decision within four weeks of completing all steps.
    Step 5

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts from the previous two years
  • Two recommendation letters (one from a math/science teacher)
  • Personal statement
  • Proof of identity and residence

Key Dates

  • Application Opens: January 15, 2024
  • Application Deadline: March 31, 2024
  • Entrance Exams: April 15-20, 2024
  • Interviews: May 1-15, 2024
  • Decision Letters: June 1, 2024
